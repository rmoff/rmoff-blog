== So speaking of talks‚Ä¶
Conferences are not just about keynotes and swag‚Äîthe reason many of us are there is to share knowledge by speaking and listening, and there were some really good talks at Current this year.
Now _obviously_ the Decodable ones were excellent üòÅ but here are just a few of the other stand-out sessions that we attended:  +
_The slides and recordings are put online by Confluent after the event so keep an eye on their website_.

* Elijah Meeks gave an excellent talk on  link:https://events.bizzabo.com/599116/agenda/session/1336676[Visualization in Motion: How to Create Effective Data Visualization with Real-Time Data] . It was both interesting as a subject‚Äîand something I‚Äôd love to see more discussion of at conferences like Current‚Äîand expertly delivered in a really engaging manner
* Sanath Shetty and Ashish Vijaywargiya from JPMorgan talked about  link:https://events.bizzabo.com/599116/agenda/session/1336691[Modernizing Systems with Kafka Connect] . Connect is a technology close to my heart and I really enjoyed the real-world validation of the success of it at scale in a large organization. They have over 1000 connector instances and use it to migrate from old Sybase systems, via SQL Server and then Debezium on Kafka Connect to more modern RDBMS using the Kafka Connect JDBC Sink.
* Joseph Thaidigsman and Thomas Thornton shared their experiences from  link:https://events.bizzabo.com/599116/agenda/session/1336731[adopting Debezium and Kafka Connect at Slack] . With 1400 Connect tasks for Debezium connector for Vitess, they run at an impressive scale, using Debezium as part of the realtime data streaming pipeline for updating their data lake. It‚Äôs just great to see adoption stories like this, and you definitely should check out the recording of this talk once it is available.
* Leonard Xu, Apache Flink PMC Member and Flink CDC Lead from Alibaba Cloud provided  link:https://events.bizzabo.com/599116/agenda/session/1336739[a deep dive into the fundamental design and implementation of Flink CDC] . He discussed many important aspects of building real-time data pipelines including the challenging problem of schema evolution to synchronize upstream schema changes with downstream systems.
