Oracle issued their quarterly link:http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/topics/security/cpujul2015-2367936.html[Critical Patch Update] yesterday, and with it notice of several security issues of note:


* The most serious for OBIEE (link:https://web.nvd.nist.gov/view/vuln/detail?vulnId=CVE-2013-2186[CVE-2013-2186]) rates 7.5 (out of 10) on the link:http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/topics/security/advisorymatrixglossary-101807.html[CVSS] scale, affecting the OBIEE Security Platform on both 11.1.1.7 and 11.1.1.9. The access vector is by the network, there's no authentication required, and it can _partially_ affect confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

** The patch for users of OBIEE 11.1.1.7 is to install the latest patchset, 11.1.1.7.150714 (3GB, released - by no coincidence I'm sure - just yesterday too).
** For OBIEE 11.1.1.9 there is a small patch (64Kb), number 21235195.
+
* There's also an issue affecting BI Mobile on the iPad _prior to 11.1.1.7_, the impact being partial impact on integrity.
* For users of ODI DQ 11.1.1.3 there's a whole slew of issues, fixed in CPU patch 21418574.
* Exalytics users who are on ILOM versions earlier that 3.2.6 are also affected by two issues (one of which is 10/10 on the link:http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/topics/security/advisorymatrixglossary-101807.html[CVSS] scale)


The CPU document also notes that it is the *final patch date for 10.1.3.4.2*. If you are still on 10g, now link:http://www.rittmanmead.com/consulting/obiee-11g-services/[really is the time to upgrade]!


Full details of the issues can be found in link:http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/topics/security/cpujul2015-2367936.html[Critical Patch Update] document, and information about patches on My Oracle Support, link:https://support.oracle.com/epmos/faces/DocContentDisplay?id=2005667.1[DocID 2005667.1].
