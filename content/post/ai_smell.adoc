It's now a joy to simply read _any_ blog post that's not AI-generated.

<!--more-->

If you actually have a good thing to write about, for the love of your readers and the good of the internet, please just write about it.
Claude might shit out a completed article but it'll stink.
Just like boomer art, readers are recognising this crap at a hundred yards and running away from it.
It's horrible to read, it makes me feel gross just opening it, and it's going straight in `/dev/null`.

image:/images/2026/05/matrix-morpheus.gif[Morpheus from The Matrix grimacing with caption "IT'S THE SMELL."]

Use AI to _refine_ what you want to say, but I (and I'm sure many others) would rather read something imperfect-but-real than a pristine-but-anodyne article that's in the same style as every other bloody post these days.
