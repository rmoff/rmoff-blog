.tl;dr
[TIP]
====
* I use AI heavily on this blog.
* I don't use AI to write any content.
====

---

_As any followers of my blog will have seen recently, I am link:/categories/ai/[a big fan] of the link:/2026/01/27/reflections-of-a-developer-on-llms-in-january-2026/[productivity]—and enjoyment—that AI can bring to one's work._
_(In fact, link:/2026/03/06/ai-will-fuck-you-up-if-youre-not-on-board/[I firmly believe] that to opt out of using AI is a somewhat negative step to take in terms of one's career.)_

*Here's how I don't use AI, _and never will_*:

* Writing content.
Full stop.
Period.
Mic drop.

Here's how I do use AI:

* *Claude is my webdev*.
It implemented the https://github.com/rmoff/rmoff-blog/commit/c88f0532a1fcfcf8a96dc497123d26d07eddee3e[new design recently], built a https://github.com/rmoff/rmoff-blog/commit/e722fe40ebcdd02121291277070d56b7297b4183[funky 404 page], migrated all my https://github.com/rmoff/rmoff-blog/commit/cb2eb0e479c91be90d3e60eb422f1a74356091b0[old WordPress content], link:/2026/01/27/cosplaying-as-a-webdev-with-claude-code-in-january-2026/[built a new site to host my conference talks], and https://github.com/rmoff/rmoff-blog/pulls?q=is:pr+Claude[much more]!
* *Claude is my https://github.com/rmoff/rmoff-blog/blob/main/.claude/skills/proofread-blog/SKILL.md[proofreader]*.
Reviewing blog post drafts is one of the first things I used LLMs for, as it enables me to keep up my posting velocity whilst improving the quality of what gets published.
I still have to run it several times to flush out even some obvious typos, but LLMs have got _markedly_ better at reviewing the actual content of my posts and challenging sections.
